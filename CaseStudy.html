Introduction

Did you know that 4 billion tons of food is produced per year and 1.3 billion tons of it goes to waste, while there are almost 1 bliion people who go to bed hungry and about 350 million who are mal-nourished. Sad to think about really. And the root cause of it is Capitalism. if money isn't being made, another route is taken. And that is why so many different communities are suffering from these food systems. A profit always need to be made. And that's why there is so much inflation and food that was once cost-effective and accessible is now becoming less cost-effective and still accessible. But no-one is able to buy them. And as a result profit-margins decrease which then leads to price increase or inflation. And so with this I am creating my own hydroponic system of growing plants without soil and hoping to help people to be inspired to do the same thing and help with the economic unstability and also food distribution that plagues our country. We have a vaiety of different plants that we can grow such as tomatoes, cilantro, lettuce, kale. And even plants that aren't native to the region you may be in as well. And this is all to help better our food systems that are in place and to help raise awareness of the Capitalism problem we have running rampart through our country.
