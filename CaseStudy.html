Habanero World Tree

Is Capitalism running rampant through your'e community? Are food prices skyrocketing and doesn't look as if they are gonna stop? Well do I have a solution for you!

Introduction

Did you know that 4 billion tons of food is produced per year and 1.3 billion tons of it goes to waste, while there are almost 1 billion people who go to bed hungry and about 350 million who are mal-nourished. Sad to think about really. The root cause of it is Capitalism. If money isn't being made, another route is taken. And that is why so many different communities are suffering from these food systems. A profit always need to be made. That's why there is so much inflation and food that was once cost-effective and accessible is now becoming less cost-effective and still accessible. But no-one is able to buy them. And as a result profit margins decrease which then leads to price increase or inflation. And so with this I am creating my own hydroponic system of growing plants without soil and hoping to help people to be inspired to do the same thing and help with the economic unstability and also the severe lack of food distribution that plagues our country. We have a variety of different plants that we can grow such as tomatoes, cilantro, lettuce, kale. And even plants that aren't native to the region you may be in as well. And this is all to help better our food systems that are in place and to help raise awareness of the Capitalism problem we have running rampant through our country.
